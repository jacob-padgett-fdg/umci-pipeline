How do I map printers?

I logged in on a new computer and I don't have any printers! How can I print?

Open My Computer and in the address bar, type \\servername, servername being whatever your local server is. \\jupiter, \\fife, \\swedish, etc.

Then right click on the printer you want to map and click connect...

It may look like it didn't do anything (it happens pretty quick usually) so go to Start -> Settings -> Printers and you should have a new printer there.

Right click and "set as default" to whatever printer you want to...shock/surprise... be your default.

How do I connect WebOutlook?

WebOutlook will run best in IE (shock, surprise) but will work in Firefox/other browsers fine.

If you are doing this from a non UMC network (home/hotel), first connect to UMC VPN.

While connected to the UMC VPN, go to http://172.16.116.75/exchange

From jobsites or even on the main network, you can go to http://ackerman/exchange

When asked for a username and password enter it as umc\<username> and your normal password.

Just logged in to a computer for the first time? Outlook doesn't work?

Click on the Start button, then Settings, then Control Panel. Double click the Mail control panel.

Click on Email Accounts.

Next...

Double click on MS Exchange Server.

Enter your username and then click Check Name. Your full name should fill in underline as well as the server name. Click Next. Click Finish..

Double click on the Outlook Address Book to get it's properties. Remove any existing Address Books. Click Close.

Click OK and close the Control Panel. Run Outlook. It will complain about "locations have been moved.... blah blah blah". Just click yes.

Right click on Contacts, Properties. Click on Outlook Address Book tab. Check the box if not already selected. Click OK.

Go to the Tools menu, select Services. Then choose the Addressing tab.

Click Add... and scoll down to the bottom of the list. Double click on Contacts. Click close, then ok.

Doing all of this is necessary only the first time you log on to a machine. This also enables you to type in a "friendly" name in the To: field and have it search your addressbook for that person.
